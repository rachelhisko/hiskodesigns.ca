Seafood Eats

Global Goals

The Concept

Global Goals is an organization that is putting forth 193 world leaders to sustainably develop our world. There are 17 goals that these leaders agreed on conquering. One that caught my eye is known as “Life Below Water.” This goal had many different targets that were created in a timeline. One of them being to, “sustainably manage and protect marine and coastal ecosystems to avoid significant adverse impacts, by strengthening their resilience, and take action for their restoration in order to achieve healthy and productive oceans.” Right now, our oceans are undergoing a major habitat loss. Humans are making it more difficult for our oceans to supply our exponential needs. Since the ocean’s coastal areas are so closely involved with the human population, it suffers more deeply, mainly as a result of manmade environmental degradation.

Currently urban professionals are in need of a way to locate restaurants that serve sustainable seafood because they are unaware of the ecological abuse that producers and consumers are contributing to our waters. Seafood eats is a restaurant locator application that allows urban professionals to distinguish which restaurants serve sustainable seafood.

Personas

Urban Professionals:

  • People who would enjoy trying new foods and restaurants.
  • People who would like eco-friendly food options.
  • People who want to change their habits to a more sustainable lifestyle.
  • People who are up-to-date with the latest technology and the world’s news.
  • People who want to live fashionable, and have trendy lifestyles.
  • People who are active in their community.
  • People who care about appearance and health.

    • The Process

    Visual Design

    Locate, Eat and Receive

    Locate a specific restaurant near them that serves sustainable seafood. Once you eat at such restaurant, check in and receive cash back!

    Rewards!

    Take pictures of your restaurant bill and receive cash back rewards that you can spend on your next visit. You are also able to see what ocean your seafood came and how it is sustainable.

    Redeem

    You are able to spend your earned cash at participating restaurants and the money NEVER expiries! Participating restaurants will scan your personal QR code to redeem your cash.
