Dailey Bailey

Smart Product Prototype

The Concept

Daily Bailey will allow dog owners access to their dogs health behaviours. This app will be linked to a dog collar that can monitor the dogs; temperature, pulse, respiration and sleep. It will also give dog owners a simple way to set dates and send emails to their veterinarian about any concerns. Dog owners are in need of a way to monitor their dog’s daily activity because they feel that their dog is a part of their family.

Personas

Kendra is a nursing graduate who has just started her new career in Ottawa. She is concerned about her dog’s health as she is on her own.

The Process
Visual Design
Overveiw

Daily Bailey allows dog owners to keep a profile of their dog, so they have something to look back on. Dog owners may also see a quick analysis of their dog’s daily health behaviours.

Track Health

Dog owners can track their dogs temperature, pulse, respiration and sleep, so they will know when the right time is, to consult their veterinarian. Dog owners will be notify if there are any abnormal health behaviours.

Consult & Schedule

Dog Owners can set reminders and dates for their next vet visit, so they don’t forget. Dog owners may also consult with their veterinarian so they don’t have to constantly pay unnecessary vet bills.

Icons

Each icon is created using Google Material guidelines, they are simple and minimal and commonly used throughout a UI. They are legible and widely known amongst android users. The icons are designed to be displayed on all common platforms and displays.
