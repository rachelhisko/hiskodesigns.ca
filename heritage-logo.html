Heritage Community Fitness

Logo Redesign

Who Are We?

Heritage Community Fitness and Rehabilitation Centre is an independently owned Canadian business that operates in the town of Carleton Place. Along with the fitness centre, we supply our members with something known as The Collaborative Care Centre. Within this, we offer: chiropractic’s, concussion management, counseling, naturopathy, personal training, physiotherapy, physiatrists, registered massage therapy and sports performance training. This combination allows us to provide our members with something that is unique to our community.

Our Brand

The Heritage logo is a strong visual image that identifies within the town of Carleton Place. The logo concept displays a friendly and welcoming environment; which is something that is valued deeply. The main icon resembles a member being physically active. The motion of the character resembles the celebration of health and physical activity. The maple leaf symbolizes the Canadian heritage within the town of Carleton Place. The combination of the logo enforces the slogan of Heritage Community Fitness, which are healthy people creates healthy communities.

Typography

The typeface used for this logo is known as Proxima Nova. This typeface can be easily reproduced, resized, and still remain readable. Proxima Nova combines modern proportions with a geometric look.

Alternative Print Font

Helvetica is a good substitution for Proxima Nova. It has the same attributes and most computers are equipped with the typeface.

Web Font

The web based font that best suits Proxima Nova is known as Montserrat. This is also a modern geometric font that can be resized and still remain readable.
